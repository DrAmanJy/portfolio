About Me

DrAmanJy is a passionate gamer and aspiring web developer with a strong background in Minecraft server administration. With years of experience in the gaming community, I've honed my skills in various titles, including GTA, Destiny, Sea of Thieves, and more. My enthusiasm for gaming extends beyond casual play, as I frequently stream my gameplay on YouTube and actively participate in the Discord community.

As a dedicated moderator on multiple Discord servers and the CEO of Go Out YT, I have cultivated a deep understanding of community management and online platforms. I've also taken the initiative to create and manage my own Discord servers, demonstrating my leadership and organizational abilities.

Driven by a thirst for knowledge, I embarked on a journey to learn programming. Starting with Java, I've gradually expanded my skillset to include HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My ongoing pursuit of learning new programming languages reflects my commitment to continuous growth and development.

In addition to my online endeavors, I've gained valuable experience in Minecraft server administration. I've successfully built and managed several servers, including Ben SMP, Kingdom SMP S1,2,3, Ender Craft S1, and Myth Craft S4,5. My expertise in Minecraft server configuration, plugin management, and community engagement has been instrumental in the success of these servers.

I've also contributed to other Minecraft servers, such as Multiverse, Nasa Mukti Kandra, and more, offering my technical skills and support to various communities.

My passion for gaming, combined with my growing technical skills, makes me a versatile and dedicated individual. I'm excited to continue exploring new opportunities and contributing to the gaming and technology communities.
